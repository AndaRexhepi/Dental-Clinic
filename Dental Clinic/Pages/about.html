Our Dentalist Office

Just steps away from vibrant, central Sunny Hill on "Rruga B" you'll find a wealth of dental expertise. Our office is bright, modern, and light-filled—with great views of Mt. Rainier—and utilizes the latest technology and equipment to ensure you have a comfortable and safe visit.

Parking is available in our building, with access at the bottom of "Rruga B" on the North side. Street parking is also available on two hour parking meters on each street surrounding our location.
