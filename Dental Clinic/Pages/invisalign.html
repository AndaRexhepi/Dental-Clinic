Invisalign

A beautiful smile is the gateway to expressing your personality and enduring new opportunities. However, many people guard their smiles from the world because of the embarrassment of crooked teeth, and do not want to deal with the hassle of traditional braces. Thanks to advancements in cosmetic dentistry, Invisalign for our patients can straighten teeth comfortably and discreetly in half the time of metal braces.

Invisalign are clear, durable plastic retainers gently guiding your teeth to its appropriate alignment while those around you do not notice you are receiving orthodontic work. Using Invisalign will provide numerous benefits compared to metal brackets and wires including:

  • You can remove the aligners when you want
  • Allows you to eat and drink whatever you enjoy
  • Brush and floss normally ensuring great periodontal health
  • Virtually invisible teeth-straitening

Your Invisalign treatment will consist of a comprehensive evaluation of your mouth. Then, the doctors will take impressions of your teeth by using 3D imaging technology to customize your aligners. You will receive your smooth aligners, which will not irritate your teeth or gums.
